The Format

One Club.
Three Visions.

Every session follows the same dramatic rhythm as the rebuild show format: manager first, then player by player, then the auction pressure, then the big reveal.

01
🏟️
Lobby & Club Crisis
One device hosts all 3 players. Pass the screen between turns. A club scenario drops — the crisis summary sets the stakes before a single decision is made.
02
🃏
Management Cards
Each player draws a management card. Some force sales. Some demand big signings. One might give total freedom. Cards stay pinned on screen all game.
03
🧑‍💼
Manager Decision
Keep the current manager or fire him and enter the manager market. Three candidates. Prices attached. Bidding starts if two players want the same man.
04
⚖️
Keep or Sell
Each key position comes up one by one. Sale price shown. You choose. Sell, and three surfaced replacements appear — bottom option, middle option, top option.
05
🔨
Live Auctions
Two players want the same signing? Auction mode starts. Highest valid bid wins. Loser returns to the market immediately. Budget updates live for everyone.
06
🏆
Reveal & Vote
All three builds revealed one by one. Side-by-side comparison shown. Each player votes for their favourite — not themselves. The crowd decides the winner.

Why Rebuildr

Built for the
Drama.

🔴
Real-Time Auctions
When two players target the same signing, a live auction fires instantly. Countdown, minimum increments, live budget validation. The tension is real.
⚡
Dynamic Card System
Management cards constrain your rebuild. Finance cards swing your budget mid-game. Cards are drawn randomly and stay pinned on screen — no forgetting your restrictions.
🎯
Guided Keep/Sell Loop
The main action is never a giant database search. Position by position, player by player. Sell, and exactly three surfaced options appear for that role.
🌍
Top 5 League Data
Players from every club across the Premier League, La Liga, Bundesliga, Serie A and Ligue 1. Real names, real values, realistic transfer logic.
🧠
Realism Engine
Prices reflect age, contract length, club prestige, and destination fit. Absurd deals get blocked. Gyökeres doesn't move to League One for €3M.
🔄
Infinite Replayability
Weighted randomness means every rebuild surfaces different names. Same club, same scenario — different shortlists. The same three signings won't appear every time.
